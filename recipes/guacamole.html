Guacamole

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip, spread, or salad first developed in Mexico.

  • 2 avocados
  • peeled
  • small onion
  • tomato
  • lime
  • salt,sugar and pepper
  1. Mash avocado in bowl, stir in onion tomato and garlic. Season with lime juice,salt,sugar and pepper
  2. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend
