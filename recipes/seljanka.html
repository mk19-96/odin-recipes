Seljanka soup

seljanka is thick and sour soup of Russian origin[that is common in Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, and other states of the former Soviet Union and certain parts of the former Eastern Bloc. It was one of the most popular dishes of the former East Germany.

  • 3 big onions
  • capers
  • 3 different meat of your choice(could be saussages,raw meat)
  • tomatopaste
  • pickeld cucumbers
  • dill, black pepper and salt for seasoning
  • broth
  1. cut onions, cucumber and meats
  2. fry onion in oil and then add meats. cook it 5 minutes
  3. add tomato paste and cook it. After that add water and broth
  4. Boil it for 15 minutes then add pickled cucubmers and capers
  5. Lastly, add seasoning and serve!
